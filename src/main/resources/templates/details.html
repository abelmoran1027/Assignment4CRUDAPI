My Top Favorite Animals


Blue

This is my dog. His name is Blue. He is 4 months old and weighs 38 lbs. He is a full Golden Retriever, and he loves to play. He is friendly with all other dogs and loves people. He is a little on edge with cats, but that is mostly because cats usually do not like to horseplay with him.

We got him from a family in Lexington, NC, who could no longer keep him. I was looking for a dog at the time and was more than willing to take him in. He had a lot of health problems when I got him, but after multiple trips to the vet, he is now healthy and happy. I have always loved Golden Retrievers because my family got one before I moved out, and it was the most loyal and sweet dog ever. Her name is Winnie. He gets plenty of attention and often goes to see Winnie.
